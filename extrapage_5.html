What's todays weather?!

Photo edited with Photoshop.

The concept I want to explore with my photomontage is a current issue we are living in which is the effects of global warming and the rapid change in weather or extreme weather. The way I conveyed this message was by including an exaggerated image that I gathered from an online source of a close up of our Sun next to the earth which is covered in snow. I used an image from online as the background to demonstrate the sudden change in climate as there is a bunch of snow and even on the trees with a grey skies which could indicate more snow. The dog is a stand in for humans and has a little hat despite having fur and a phone with the weather forecasting showing the sudden change in the climate with his little paw sticking out. These images are all gathered from online sources provided by the professor which allowed me to use free source images. Furthermore, I want to convey how the dog is confused by the shift in weather from warm sunny to snowy and cold. The dog doesn't look pleased by such rapid shifts as he looks outward in disbelief. I also made the sun huge and as close to the image to draw the viewers' attention from the dog and his surroundings, I want the viewer to start from left to right and look around the image. Techniques I used to create this image that I learned from class is masking to hide portions of images I transported and selected into one image to perfectly hide portions I didn’t need while not ruining the images quality. I used this technique to hide the dogs paw as in the unmasked version the arm fur would get in the way of the dogs body but also make it obvious that the paw didn't match the fur color of the lab, so that came in handy. Another technique I used was blending modes specifically the darken option to make all my images work together and not be so obvious that each object I selected from other images have different colors. It allows the image to flow together despite the exaggerated features. The most useful feature was having photoshop perfectly crop items for me and just allowing me to drag images to my background which makes the process smooth, all these features combine allow me to express a dog suffering from climate change and how it has left the earth exposed to our harsh environmental heater.
